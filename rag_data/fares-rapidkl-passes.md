# RapidKL Travel Passes & Concession Cards

Source: Prasarana Malaysia Berhad / MyRapid / mrt.com.my
Last verified: 2024

---

## MY50 Monthly Travel Pass

- **Price**: RM 50 for 30 days of unlimited travel
- **Valid on**: LRT, MRT, BRT Sunway Line, KL Monorail, RapidKL buses,
  MRT feeder buses
- **NOT valid on**: KTM Komuter, KTM ETS, KLIA Ekspres, KLIA Transit, GoKL
- **How to activate**: Use MyKad (with Touch 'n Go function) at any
  LRT, MRT, Monorail, or BRT Customer Service Office
- **Usage**: Tap in and out as normal — the system deducts from the pass
  instead of card balance during the 30-day period
- **Balance requirement**: Must still maintain RM 3.00 minimum purse value
  on your card even with MY50 active
- **Best for**: Daily commuters making 2+ trips per day in the Klang Valley

## MyCity Pass (Tourist / Short-Stay Pass)

- **1-day pass**: RM 20 — unlimited LRT, MRT, Monorail travel for 1 day
- **3-day pass**: RM 50 — unlimited LRT, MRT, Monorail travel for 3 days
- **Valid on**: LRT, MRT, KL Monorail (rail only, not buses)
- **NOT valid on**: RapidKL buses, KTM, KLIA trains
- **Where to buy**: All RapidKL LRT, MRT, and Monorail stations
- **Best for**: Tourists or visitors making multiple journeys in 1–3 days

---

## KL TravelPass (Tourists)

- Combines unlimited rail travel with KLIA airport transfer
- **Options**:
  - Rail only + 1-way KLIA: RM 55
  - Rail only + return KLIA: RM 100
- **Valid on**: LRT, MRT, KL Monorail + KLIA Ekspres or KLIA Transit
- **Can also load cash**: Works as a stored value card for buses and retail
- **Where to buy**: KL Sentral KLIA Ekspres counters and selected stations
- **Best for**: International visitors arriving via KLIA who want seamless transit

## MyRapid Concession Card

Provides **50% discount** on all RapidKL fares (calculated on cash fare rate).

### Eligible Groups
| Group | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| Senior citizens | Malaysian, aged 60+ (MyKad required) |
| School students | Enrolled in Malaysian school (student ID) |
| Persons with disabilities | Registered with JKM / Department of Social Welfare |

### How to Apply
- Online: myrapid.com.my (Fares & Payments section)
- In person: Any RapidKL Customer Service office
- Card cost: RM 15.00 (RM 5.20 card + RM 9.80 value)
- Processing: Select collection point during application

### How It Works
- Tap in and out as normal — concession rate applied automatically
- Concession is 50% off the **cash fare rate** (not cashless rate)
  - Example: If cash fare = RM 3.60, concession = RM 1.80
  - Note: this is HIGHER than the standard cashless rate for the same journey
  - Standard cashless might be RM 2.80, concession token = RM 1.80
- Valid for all LRT, MRT, Monorail, BRT, and RapidKL buses
- One card per person, registered to MyKad

## MyRapid Touch 'n Go Card (Standard)

- **Purchase price**: RM 15.00 (RM 5.00 card deposit + RM 10.00 initial value)
  - At-station counters: RM 15.00
  - On-bus purchase: RM 10.00 (RM 5.20 card + RM 4.80 value — different pricing)
- **Works on**: LRT, MRT, Monorail, BRT, all RapidKL buses, KTM Komuter,
  tolls, parking, selected retail outlets
- **Reload locations**: 9,000+ points Malaysia-wide — stations (RM 0.50 fee),
  Touch 'n Go app (free), pharmacies, 7-Eleven, petrol stations, ATMs
- **Card validity**: Does not expire if used at least once every 5 years
- **Balance transfer**: Only possible if card was registered; unregistered cards
  cannot have balance recovered if lost

## Choosing the Right Product

| Situation | Recommended Product |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Daily KL commuter (rail + bus) | MY50 Monthly Pass (RM 50/month) |
| Tourist, 1–2 days in KL | MyCity Pass 1-day (RM 20) |
| Tourist flying into KLIA + sightseeing | KL TravelPass (from RM 55) |
| Senior citizen / student / OKU | Concession Card |
| Occasional user, all-in-one | Standard Touch 'n Go card |
| Just arrived, no card yet | Buy on bus (RM 10) or at station (RM 15) |
