Source: Prasarana Malaysia Berhad / MyRapid (myrapid.com.my)
Last verified: 2024

## Rapid KL Bus (Klang Valley)

### Fare Structure
RapidKL buses use a **zone-based cashless fare system**.
- Buses are 100% cashless — Touch 'n Go or MyKad only, no cash accepted
- Tap IN when boarding, tap OUT before disembarking
- If you do not tap out, the **maximum fare for the route** is charged automatically
- The system calculates exact fare based on zones travelled

### Zone Fares

| Zones Crossed | Fare (RM) |
|---------------|-----------|
| 1 zone        | RM 0.80 – RM 1.00 |
| 2 zones       | RM 1.20 – RM 1.50 |
| 3 zones       | RM 1.50 – RM 2.00 |
| 4+ zones      | RM 2.00 – RM 3.00 (route dependent) |

Most short city routes (within KL city): RM 1.00 flat
Suburban and feeder routes: RM 1.20 – RM 2.50 depending on distance

### MRT Feeder Buses
- Feeder buses connecting MRT stations to surrounding neighbourhoods
- Flat cashless fare: **RM 1.00 per journey**
- Must have minimum RM 3.00 balance before boarding any RapidKL bus

### GoKL Free City Bus
- 4 colour-coded circular routes within KL city centre
- Completely **free** — no card or payment required, just board
- Operating hours: 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM daily
- Routes cover: Chow Kit, KLCC, Masjid India, Masjid Jamek, Pasar Seni,
  Brickfields, KL Sentral, Bangsar

### BRT Sunway Line (Sunway/Subang area)
- Connects Sunway Pyramid area to USJ / Subang Jaya LRT
- Fare: integrated with LRT/MRT (same cashless fare table applies)
- Distance-based, not a flat bus fare

### Concession on Buses
- 50% discount for eligible groups (students, senior citizens, OKU)
- Requires registered MyRapid Concession Card
- Applied automatically when you tap in with your concession card

## Rapid Penang Bus

### Georgetown City Routes
- Flat cashless fare: **RM 1.50 per journey**
- Touch 'n Go or MyRapid card accepted

### CAT (Central Area Transit) — Free Bus
- Free circular routes in Georgetown heritage zone
- No payment required, open boarding
- Operating hours: 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM daily
- 4 routes: Red, Blue, Green, Purple — covering Georgetown, Weld Quay,
  Komtar, Penang Hill foothills

### Airport Route (Rapid Penang 401E)
- Penang International Airport ↔ Komtar (Georgetown)
- Fare: RM 4.00 cashless

### Inter-district Routes (mainland Penang / Butterworth area)
- Varies: RM 1.50 – RM 4.00 depending on distance

## Rapid Kuantan Bus (Pahang)

### City Routes
- Flat fare: **RM 1.00 – RM 2.00** depending on route
- Touch 'n Go accepted on most routes
- Some routes still accept cash — check route-specific information

## Key Rules for All RapidKL Buses

1. **Always tap OUT** before leaving the bus — failure to tap out = maximum fare charged
2. **Minimum balance**: RM 3.00 before boarding (RM 5.00 for rail)
3. **Multiple passengers on one card**: allowed, inform Bus Captain first,
   all must exit at same stop
4. **Replacement bus**: if bus breaks down, tap IN again on replacement bus —
   no double charge will occur
5. **Card purchase on bus**: available for RM 10.00 (RM 5.20 card + RM 4.80 value)
6. **No cash change**: buses do not carry cash — cashless only
