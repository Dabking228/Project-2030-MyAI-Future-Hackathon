# Malaysian Public Transport Fare Rules

This document covers fare structures for all operators in the SYBAR_AI system.
Last updated: 2026

---

## 1. Prasarana — LRT, MRT, Monorail (Klang Valley)

### LRT Kelana Jaya Line (Putra LRT)
- Distance-based fares: RM 1.20 (1–3 km) to RM 4.00 (30+ km)
- MyRapid card: 10–15% discount on all fares
- Monthly unlimited pass: RM 100 (Klang Valley zone)
- Concession: 50% discount for students, senior citizens, OKU cardholders
- First train: ~6:00 AM, Last train: ~11:30 PM (weekdays)
- Peak hours: 7:00–9:00 AM and 5:00–7:00 PM

### LRT Ampang / Sri Petaling Line
- Same distance-based fare structure as Kelana Jaya line
- Interchange at Masjid Jamek (free transfer within 30 minutes with MyRapid card)
- Interchange at Chan Sow Lin with MRT Putrajaya Line

### MRT Kajang Line (MRT1)
- Distance-based fares: RM 1.00 (1–5 km) to RM 4.50 (45+ km)
- MyRapid card discounts apply
- Connects to KTM at KL Sentral, LRT at Pasar Seni, Masjid Jamek

### MRT Putrajaya Line (MRT2)
- Distance-based fares: RM 1.20 to RM 5.10
- Interchange with LRT at Chan Sow Lin, Titiwangsa
- Connects to KLIA via KL Sentral ERL interchange

### KL Monorail (Bukit Bintang / KL Tower area)
- Flat fare: RM 1.20–RM 2.50 depending on distance
- Short urban connector: 11 stations from Brickfields to Titiwangsa
- No direct interchange with MRT — walking transfer required at KL Sentral or Titiwangsa

### Integrated Fares (touch-and-go transfers)
- If you tap out at an LRT station and tap in at an MRT station within the same
  complex within 30 minutes, no additional boarding charge applies.
- This applies at: KL Sentral, Titiwangsa, Chan Sow Lin, Pasar Seni

## 2. KTMB — KTM Komuter and Intercity

### KTM Komuter (Klang Valley urban rail)
- Port Klang–Sentul line and Batu Caves–Pulau Sebang/Algebra line
- Fare zones: Zone 1–8 based on distance
- Zone 1 (1–7 km): RM 1.00 | Zone 2 (8–15 km): RM 1.50 | Zone 3 (16–23 km): RM 2.00
- Zone 4 (24–31 km): RM 2.50 | Zone 5 (32–40 km): RM 3.00 | Zone 6 (41+ km): RM 3.50
- Monthly pass: RM 150 (full Komuter network)
- First train: 5:30 AM, Last train: 11:00 PM

### KTM ETS (Electric Train Service — intercity)
- Ipoh–KL Sentral–Gemas corridor
- ETS Platinum (fastest): Higher fare, limited stops
- ETS Gold: Standard express service
- Fares range from RM 14 (KL Sentral–Rawang) to RM 90+ (KL Sentral–Padang Besar)
- Booking required for ETS — available at ktmb.com.my or station counters
- No advance booking needed for Komuter

## 3. BAS.MY Stage Bus Operators

### Johor Bahru (Causeway Link / Mbf Holdings)
- Flat fare: RM 1.00 within city zone
- Suburban routes: RM 1.50–RM 3.00 depending on distance
- JB Sentral bus terminal is the main hub

### Melaka (Panorama Melaka)
- Flat fare: RM 1.50 (all routes within Melaka Tengah)
- Tourist areas (Jonker Street, A Famosa): RM 2.00 flat

### Ipoh (Perak Transit)
- Flat fare: RM 1.00–RM 2.00 within city
- Suburban: RM 2.50–RM 4.00

### Seremban (Seremban Bus)
- Flat fare: RM 1.00–RM 2.00

### Rapid Penang
- Flat fare: RM 1.50 (city) to RM 4.00 (airport route)
- CAT (free bus): Free circular route in Georgetown

## 4. MyRapid Card (Touch 'n Go transit card)

### Accepted On
- All Prasarana services: LRT, MRT, Monorail, Rapid KL bus
- NOT accepted on KTM (uses own monthly pass or cash at gates)
- BAS.MY buses: varies — some accept Touch 'n Go, some cash only

### Balance Requirements
- Minimum balance to board: RM 0.50
- Recommended top-up amount: RM 20–RM 50
- Auto top-up available via bank direct debit

### Concession Cards
- Student card: 50% discount — requires student ID registration at MyRapid office
- Senior citizen: 50% discount — MyKad registration required
- OKU (disabled): Free travel on Prasarana — separate OKU card required

## 5. Free Services

- GoKL City Bus: Free circular routes in KL city centre (4 colour-coded lines)
  - Red Line: Puduraya–Masjid India–Chow Kit–Titiwangsa
  - Blue Line: Puduraya–Brickfields–KL Sentral
  - Green Line: Puduraya–Brickfields–Bangsar
  - Purple Line: Masjid Jamek–Brickfields–KL Sentral–Bangsar
- CAT Bus Penang: Free in Georgetown
