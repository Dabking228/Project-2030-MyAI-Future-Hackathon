# Malaysian Public Transport Service Policies

This document covers operating hours, service disruption procedures,
ticketing rules, accessibility, and luggage policies for all Malaysian
transit operators in the SYBAR_AI system.

---

### Operating Hours

| Line | First Train (Weekday) | Last Train (Weekday) | Weekend |
|------|-----------------------|----------------------|---------|
| LRT Kelana Jaya | 6:00 AM | 11:55 PM | 6:00 AM – 11:55 PM |
| LRT Ampang/Sri Petaling | 6:00 AM | 11:55 PM | 6:00 AM – 11:55 PM |
| MRT Kajang (SBK) | 6:00 AM | 11:55 PM | 6:00 AM – 11:55 PM |
| MRT Putrajaya (PY) | 6:00 AM | 11:55 PM | 6:00 AM – 11:55 PM |
| KL Monorail | 6:00 AM | 11:55 PM | 6:00 AM – 11:55 PM |
| Rapid KL Bus (most routes) | 6:00 AM | 11:00 PM | Reduced frequency |

Public holidays: Same as Sunday schedule (reduced frequency, normal hours).

### Service Frequency

| Time | LRT/MRT Headway | Bus Headway |
|------|-----------------|-------------|
| Peak (7–9 AM, 5–7 PM weekdays) | 3–5 minutes | 10–15 minutes |
| Off-peak (daytime) | 5–8 minutes | 20–30 minutes |
| Late night (after 10 PM) | 8–12 minutes | 30–45 minutes |
| Weekend | 5–8 minutes | 20–40 minutes |

### Service Disruption Procedures
- Check real-time status at: myrapid.com.my or the Rapid KL official app
- During disruptions, Prasarana deploys free shuttle buses between affected stations
- Shuttle buses stop at the main entrance of each affected station
- Announcements are made in Malay and English inside all stations
- Refunds for disruptions: apply at any Customer Service counter within 7 days
  with your Touch 'n Go card (credit is returned to card balance)

### Luggage and Bicycle Policy
- Standard luggage: allowed if it fits in the overhead rack or under the seat
- Maximum luggage size: 70 cm × 50 cm × 25 cm
- Bicycles: NOT permitted on LRT/MRT/Monorail during peak hours
  (7:00–9:30 AM and 4:30–7:30 PM weekdays)
- Folding bicycles (folded): permitted at all times in designated areas
- Food and drinks: covered containers only, no hot food on trains

### Accessibility
- All Prasarana stations have lifts, ramps, and tactile guidance
- Wheelchair-accessible entry at all stations (some older LRT stations: one accessible gate only)
- Blue priority seating: front of each carriage for elderly, disabled, pregnant passengers
- OKU (disabled) MyRapid card: free travel, apply at Rapid KL Customer Service

## 2. KTMB (KTM Komuter and Intercity)

### Operating Hours — KTM Komuter

| Route | First Train | Last Train |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| Port Klang – Sentul | 5:30 AM | 11:35 PM |
| Batu Caves – Pulau Sebang/Algebra | 5:35 AM | 11:20 PM |
| KL Sentral – Seremban (via Kajang) | 5:45 AM | 11:00 PM |

Weekend: Same hours, frequency reduced by ~30%.

### Service Frequency — KTM Komuter
- Peak hours: 10–15 minutes
- Off-peak: 20–30 minutes
- Late night: 30–45 minutes

### KTM ETS / Intercity Booking Policy
- Advance booking strongly recommended for ETS (especially weekends and holidays)
- Book online at ktmb.com.my or at station counters (up to 60 days in advance)
- Walk-up tickets available but may sell out for popular routes
- No-show policy: ticket is forfeited, no refund without prior cancellation
- Cancellation: full refund if cancelled >3 hours before departure; 50% if <3 hours

### KTM Service Disruptions
- Check: ktmb.com.my or KTMB official social media (Twitter/X: @ktmbmalaysia)
- Disruption announcements made via PA system and station display boards
- No replacement shuttle buses during KTMB disruptions (unlike Prasarana)
- Refunds: apply at originating station counters

### KTM Luggage Policy
- KTM Komuter: Same as Prasarana (carry-on sized bags, no large luggage)
- KTM ETS/Intercity: Luggage rack available, maximum 20 kg per person
- Bicycles: NOT permitted on ETS; permitted on some Komuter services off-peak only

- First bus: 6:00 AM (most routes)

## 4. MyRapid Touch 'n Go Card Rules

### Card Validity
- Card does not expire as long as it is used at least once every 5 years
- Balance does not expire
- Lost/damaged card: balance cannot be transferred without original receipt
  (strongly recommended to register card online for recovery)

### Top-Up Locations
- All LRT/MRT/Monorail station kiosks and counters
- Touch 'n Go reload kiosks at major shopping centres
- Online via myTNG app (bank transfer, credit/debit card)
- Selected 7-Eleven, MyNews, and petrol stations
- Auto top-up: minimum RM 10 to maximum RM 50 per auto-reload transaction

### Minimum Balance to Board
- LRT/MRT/Monorail: RM 0.50 (system blocks if below)
- Rapid KL Bus: RM 0.50
- If balance is too low, top up at kiosks before entering gantry

### Card Blocking and Recovery
- Card blocked if used more than 5 times in 2 hours on the same route (anti-fraud)
- Contact Rapid KL Customer Service at 03-7885 2585 to unblock
- Student/senior/OKU concession: only one card per person, registered to MyKad

## 6. KLIA Ekspres / KLIA Transit (ERL)

### Operating Hours
- KLIA Ekspres (non-stop KL Sentral ↔ KLIA): 5:00 AM – 1:00 AM daily
- KLIA Transit (stops at Salak Tinggi, Putrajaya): same hours
- Frequency: every 15 minutes (KLIA Ekspres), every 20 minutes (KLIA Transit)

### Fares (KLIA Ekspres)
- KL Sentral → KLIA: RM 55 (adult one-way)
- KL Sentral → KLIA2: RM 55 (adult one-way)
- Return ticket: RM 100
- Group discount (4+ passengers): RM 50 per person

### Booking
- Buy at KL Sentral ERL counters, self-service kiosks, or online at kliaekspres.com
- Touch 'n Go card NOT accepted — separate ERL ticket required
- Journey time: 28 minutes (KLIA Ekspres, non-stop)

## 7. Disruption Handling — General Advice

When a line is disrupted, SYBAR_AI will suggest alternatives using the
current GTFS real-time data. In general:

- **LRT disruption**: Use Rapid KL bus (slower, same coverage area) or
  connecting MRT line if available at a nearby interchange
- **KTM disruption**: No automatic replacement — consider taxi/e-hailing
  or wait for service to resume (usually within 1–2 hours for minor faults)
- **Bus disruption**: Check next service time; bus disruptions are common
  and typically resolved within 30–60 minutes
- Always check operator official apps for real-time status before travelling
