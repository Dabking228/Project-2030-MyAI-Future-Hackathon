## Fare Structure Overview

RapidKL rail uses a **distance-based integrated fare system**.
Fares are calculated based on the total journey distance across ALL lines
boarded in a single trip. Transferring between LRT, MRT, Monorail, and BRT
within the integrated network does NOT add a new boarding charge —
only the total origin-to-destination distance determines the fare.

Two fare tiers exist:
- **Cashless fare** (Touch 'n Go card / MyKad / MyRapid card): Lower rate
- **Cash fare** (Single Journey Token purchased at TVM): Higher rate

## Cash Fare (Single Journey Token)

Cash fares are approximately **20–30% higher** than cashless fares for the same journey.
Tokens are sold at Token Vending Machines (TVMs) at all LRT, MRT, Monorail, and BRT stations.
Concession tokens (50% discount) are available from Customer Service officers only.

Minimum cash fare: RM 1.80
Maximum cash fare: RM 8.90 (longest journeys)

## Integrated Transfer Rules

- Transfers between LRT, MRT, Monorail, and BRT Sunway Line are **free of extra boarding charge**
  when using the same Touch 'n Go card, provided the transfer is completed within the system
- The fare is calculated based on the total end-to-end distance of the full journey
- If you exit through a paid gate (tap out) and re-enter at another station, a new journey begins
  and a new fare is charged — avoid unnecessary exits during transfers
- Transfer time limit: complete your transfer within the station complex; there is no explicit
  time window but you must not exit to street level and re-enter

## Minimum Balance Requirements

- Must have at least **RM 5.00** purse value before boarding any rail service
- If balance drops below RM 5.00, top up before entering the fare gate
- Top-up available at all station Customer Service counters (RM 0.50 service fee),
  self-service reload kiosks, Touch 'n Go app, or over 9,000 reload points Malaysia-wide

## Concession Fares (50% Discount)

Eligible groups:
- Senior citizens (Malaysian, aged 60 and above) — MyKad required
- School students (registered in Malaysia) — student ID required
- Persons with disabilities / OKU (registered with JKM) — OKU card required

Concession discount applies to the **cash fare rate**, not the cashless rate.
Apply for a Concession Card at any RapidKL Customer Service office or online at myrapid.com.my.
